Question 1.
Do you have the passion to do something other than your studies like these two icons have?

Ans. My passion is Kuchipudi dance.

Question 2.
Do you find any difficulty in pursuing your passion?

Ans. Kuchipudi is considered one of the most difficult classical dances in India. The tricky part is to express all the Navarasas and the four Abhinayas in a way the audience can understand. Another thing that I find difficult is to show a fierce masculine moment.

Ans. It's my parents and my teacher who support and encourage me to go ahead with my passion.
